What matters..

Posted on September 10, 2017 by rmcdonell by rmcdonell

Success is not just measured by how much you accumulate.  Its also how much you give.  I’ve spent much of my life just floating.  Now in my middle years I am deciding to start swimming.  Is it too late? Have I wasted a lot of time?  I’ve asked myself this question many times and the answer is always the same. “How bad do you want it?”

You don’t create life, you discover it as you go.
