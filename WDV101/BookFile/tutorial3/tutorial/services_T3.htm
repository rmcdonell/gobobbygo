Getting Around Town

Wherever you need to go, Classic Taxi is ready to take you there, day or night. We service downtown Toronto and the Greater Toronto Area as well.

Universities and Colleges

Classic Taxi services the University of Toronto, Ryerson University and George Brown College with safe rides to students 24/7 for errands, work, late night studying, or nighttime entertainment. Discounts are available to students who show current student ID to the driver.

Tours

We offer sightseeing tours of downtown and historic Toronto. Tours are a minimum of 1 hour and maximum of 2 hours. Call us to customize your tour.
